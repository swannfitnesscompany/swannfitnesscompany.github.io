The Coach

Swann Running is a multi-generational running team consisting of members of different abilities and aspirations that range from very competitive atheletes to those who run to lose weight and gain fitness. We all want to share our passion for life and running, our experiences, and the lessons we are learing along the way. We each run for a variety of reasons, and we each have different goals, but we are here to inspire, encorage, and challenge each other to be the best runnner and human being we can be.
